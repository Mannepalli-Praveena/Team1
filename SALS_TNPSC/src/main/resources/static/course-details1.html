Combined Civil Services Examination – I (Group 1 Services)

20,000

Course Overview

Eligibility
* TNPSC Group 1 Age Limit – General category 32 years, age relaxation of 5 years to SC, ST, OBC category
* TNPSC Group 1 Qualification – Graduation from a Government recognized university
* Special physical qualifications are needed for the posts of Deputy Superintendent of Police and the District Officer (Fire and Rescue Operations).

Exam Pattern
* Prelims – one objective paper – 300 marks – 3 hours
* Mains – three descriptive papers – 750 marks total – 9 hours total
* Interview – 100 marks

Syllabus
* Prelims Syllabus
- General Studies – 150 questions (Degree Standard)
-Aptitude & Mental Ability – 50 questions (SSLC Standard)

* Mains Syllabus
- Paper 1 – Modern Indian History and Indian Culture Social Issues – India and Tamil Nadu General Aptitude & Mental Ability (SSLC Standard)
- Paper 2 – Indian Polity and Emerging Political Trends Role and Impact of Science and Technology in India Tamil Society – Culture and Heritage
- Paper 3 – Indian Geography – Tamil Nadu Biodiversity, Disaster TNPSC - Group 1, 2, 3 & 4 Tamil Nadu PSC Exams 8/21 Management Indian Economy – Current trends
