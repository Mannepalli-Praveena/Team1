How to Apply for TNPSC Exams and Posts


Welcome to the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission (TNPSC) online application portal. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to apply for TNPSC exams and posts:

Registration Process:
1.Visit the official TNPSC website at TNPSC
2.Click on the "One-Time Registration" link to create your TNPSC account.
3.Fill in the required personal details accurately, including your name, date of birth, address, email ID, and mobile number.
4.Choose a unique username and password for your account.
5.After successful registration, you will receive a confirmation email or SMS with your login credentials.

Application Form:
1.Log in to your TNPSC account using your username and password.
2.Navigate to the "Apply Online" section and select the desired exam or post you wish to apply for.
3.Carefully read the instructions and eligibility criteria before proceeding.
4.Fill out the online application form with accurate details, including educational qualifications, work experience (if applicable), and other required information.
5.Upload scanned copies of your photograph and signature in the prescribed format.
6.Review the filled-in application form for any errors or omissions before final submission.

Fee Payment
1.Pay the prescribed application fee using one of the available payment methods, such as online banking, debit/credit card, or through authorized e-payment centers.
2.The application fee varies depending on the exam or post you are applying for and your category (General, OBC, SC/ST, etc.).
3.Ensure that you complete the fee payment process successfully to avoid any delays in application processing.

Submission
1.Once you have filled in the application form and paid the fee, click on the "Submit" button to complete the application process.
2.Take a printout of the submitted application form and fee payment receipt for your records.

Contact Information

If you encounter any issues or have questions regarding the process, please contact TNPSC's helpline at [1800 419 0958] or email us at [helpdesk@tnpscexams.in].

NOTE:

1.Candidates are advised to read the official notification and instructions carefully before applying.
2.Incomplete or incorrect applications are liable to be rejected.
3.Candidates are responsible for providing accurate information and documents during the application process.
Thank you for choosing TNPSC for your career aspirations. We wish you the best of luck with your application!
