Download Hall Ticket

Instructions:
Step 1: Log in to the official website of the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission (TNPSC).TNPSC
Step 2: Navigate to the "Hall Ticket Download" or "Admit Card" section.
Step 3: Enter your application number and date of birth as per the registration details.
Step 4: Click on the "Download Hall Ticket" button.
Step 5: Save or print your hall ticket for future reference.

Contact Information
If you encounter any issues or have questions regarding the process, please contact TNPSC's helpline at [1800 419 0958] or email us at [helpdesk@tnpscexams.in].

NOTE:
Candidates are advised to read the official notification and instructions carefully.
